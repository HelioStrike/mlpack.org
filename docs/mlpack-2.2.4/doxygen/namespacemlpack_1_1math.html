Upper triangular representation of a symmetric matrix, scaled such that, dot(Svec(A), Svec(B)) == dot(A, B) for symmetric A, B.

Specifically,

Svec(K) = [ K_11, sqrt(2) K_12, ..., sqrt(2) K_1n, K_22, ..., sqrt(2) K_2n, ..., K_nn ]^T
